Expenditure on Military

This analysis about Expenditure of Military has been calculated from the data we collected from World Bank.

Data despription

The graphs below covered the 10 countries: Australia, United States, South Africa, Argentina, Brazil, Mexico, France, Germany, Italy and Indonesia, the years covered from 2011 to 2016.





Expenditure on Military in absolute value (USD)

From this graph, we can see the United States is the country which spends the most money on the Military, and Canada is which spends the least.

Expenditure on Military in the percentage of GDP

From this graph, we can see United States is the country which spends the highest percentage of the GDP on the Military, and Japan is which spends the least percentage of the GDP.

Expenditure on Military in absolute value of per Capita (USD)
The growth of expenditure on Military in absolutely value (2011 - 2016)

From this graph, we can see that China increases expenditure far more than other countries on the Military, and the United States decreases the expenditure on Military the most.

The growth of expenditure on Military in % GDP (2011 - 2016)

From this graph, we can see that China is the country which increase the most percenatage of the GDP on the Military, and the United States is the country which decreases the most percentage of the GDP on it.
