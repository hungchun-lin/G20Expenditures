Expenditure on Education

This analysis about Expenditure of Education has been calculated from the data we collected from World Bank.

Data despription

The graphs below covered the 10 countries: Australia, United States, South Africa, Argentina, Brazil, Mexico, France, Germany, Italy and Indonesia, the years covered from 2011 to 2016.


*Every country of G20 is missing the data of Expenditure on Education in 2017.



Expenditure on Education in absolute value (USD)

From this graph, we can see the United States is the country which spends the most money on the Education, and India is which spends the least.

Expenditure on Education in the percentage of GDP

From this graph, we can see Brazil is the country which spends the highest percentage of the GDP on the Education, and China is which spends the least percentage of the GDP.

Expenditure on Education in absolute value of per Capita (USD)
The expenditure on Education vs Military

This graph shows that the expenditure on Education is always higher than the expenditure on Military.

The growth of expenditure on Education in absolutely value (2011 - 2016)

From this graph, we can see that China increases the expenditure on the Education the most, and Japan decreases the expenditure on Education the most.

The growth of expenditure on Education in % GDP (2011 - 2016)

From this graph, we can see that Brazil increases the percentage of the GDP on the Education the most, and United State decreases the expenditure on Education the most.
